The whole app, in one idea

It's 11pm. The craving hits. Instead of willpower alone, you reach into your pocket and tap one button.

1️⃣  Name it & rate it — 5 seconds
2️⃣  Watch the wave rise & fall while you breathe
3️⃣  Tap a quick action that works for you
4️⃣  See your reason, in your own words
5️⃣  Close the loop — no shame either way

Everything else — insights, streaks, savings — fills in automatically from these moments. You never have to "remember to log."
